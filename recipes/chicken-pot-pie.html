Chicken Pot Pie

Description

A delicious chicken pie made from scratch with carrots, peas, and celery in a pre-made crust. Add thyme and poultry seasoning for more flavor.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Vegetables
    • Carrots
    • Onions
    • Celery
    • Peas
  • Butter and Flour
  • Seasoning
    • Salt
    • Pepper
  • Chicken Broth and Milk
  • Pie Crust

Steps

  1. Make the Filling
    • Boil cubed cut chicken with vegetables for about 15 min
    • Drain and set aside
    • Cook onions with butter until clear then stir with flour and seasoning
    • Add chicken broth and milk until filling is thick
  2. Fill and Bake Pie
